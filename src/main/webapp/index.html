Math Talent Search Programs

the math talent search program is a non-profit program helping K-12 kids explore and foster a love for mathematics. We realize that mathematically gifted students show their strength in many ways. Some may be gifted in fast problem-solving; some may be in research or recreational math. Some enjoys the competitive atmosphere, while other not. The program aims at inspiring a life-long curiosity for mathematics by creatively solving math problems or discovering math ideas or laws from an early age. Borrowed ideas from many existing projects of similar kind, such as the Wisconsin math talent search, and the Global math project, we currently offer two ways to help kids identify their math capacity.
